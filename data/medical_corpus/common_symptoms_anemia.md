# Common Symptoms Associated with Anemia

## Frequently Reported Symptoms
- Persistent fatigue
- Reduced exercise tolerance
- Dizziness or lightheadedness
- Shortness of breath on exertion

## Symptom Severity
Severity depends on:
- Degree of hemoglobin reduction
- Speed of onset
- Underlying health conditions

## Clinical Importance
Symptoms guide urgency but do not replace lab interpretation.

## Disclaimer
Symptoms vary among individuals and require medical evaluation.
