## What is a CBC?
A Complete Blood Count (CBC) is a common blood test that evaluates overall health and detects a variety of disorders.

## Key Components
- Hemoglobin
- Hematocrit
- Red Blood Cell count
- White Blood Cell count
- Platelet count

## Why CBC is Ordered
- Evaluate anemia
- Detect infection
- Monitor chronic medical conditions
- Assess bone marrow function

## Interpretation Notes
CBC values should always be interpreted together and in clinical context.

## Specialist Involved
- General Physician
- Hematologist (if abnormalities persist)

## Disclaimer
CBC interpretation requires clinical correlation by a healthcare professional.
