## What are Free T4 and T3?
Free T4 and T3 are thyroid hormones that regulate metabolism, energy, and growth.

## Relationship with TSH
- High TSH with low Free T4 suggests hypothyroidism
- Low TSH with high Free T4/T3 suggests hyperthyroidism

## Why These Tests Matter
They help clarify thyroid function when TSH is abnormal.

## Specialist Involved
- Endocrinologist

## Educational Note
Lab values must be interpreted by a qualified clinician.
