## Hemoglobin-Related Red Flags
- Very low hemoglobin values
- Symptoms of chest pain, fainting, or severe breathlessness

## Thyroid-Related Red Flags
- Extremely high or low thyroid hormone levels
- Altered mental status
- Cardiac rhythm abnormalities

## Clinical Importance
Red flags help prioritize medical evaluation but do not replace professional judgment.

## Disclaimer
Urgency decisions must be made by healthcare professionals.
