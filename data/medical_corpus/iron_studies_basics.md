## What are Iron Studies?
Iron studies are blood tests that assess iron metabolism in the body.

## Common Tests Included
- Serum iron
- Ferritin
- Total Iron Binding Capacity (TIBC)
- Transferrin saturation

## Why Iron Studies are Ordered
- Evaluate iron deficiency anemia
- Assess iron overload
- Monitor response to therapy

## Specialist Involved
- General Physician
- Hematologist

## Educational Disclaimer
These tests must be interpreted in conjunction with clinical findings.
